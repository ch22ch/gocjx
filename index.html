Features

Everything your agency needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built by someone who actually works in an insurance agency. Every feature solves a real daily problem.

Click-to-dial calling

One click in CJX rings your desk phone, then connects your client. Call logs, notes, and contact info — all in one place.

Two-way SMS

Text clients from your agency number. Full conversation history, real-time delivery, and contact name resolution.

Quote queue

Incoming quote requests land in a shared queue. Claim, assign, and track — with HO, AUTO, BOP, BAP, CPP, Farm, PU, and CU built in.

Contact notes & CRM

Every call, every note, every policy detail — attached to the client. Full context for whoever picks up next.

Team management

Real-time availability, extensions, role-based permissions, and internal messaging. Admins see everything.

Enterprise security

2FA, encrypted data, role-based access, audit logging, and account lockout. Built to compliance standards.
