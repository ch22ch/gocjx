Features
Everything your agency needs.
Nothing it doesn't.
Built by someone who actually works in an insurance agency. Every feature exists because it solves a real daily problem.

WebRTC Calling

Make and receive calls directly in the browser. No desk phones needed. Call logging, transfer, hold, and mute built in.

SMS Messaging

Text clients from your agency number. Full conversation history, read receipts, and quick replies. No personal phones.

Quick Quote Queue

Incoming quote requests land in a shared queue. Claim, assign, and track — no more lost sticky notes or email chains.

Contact Notes

Every call, every note, every policy detail — attached to the client. The next person who picks up the phone has full context.

Team Management

See who's available, busy, or away. Internal calling and messaging. Role-based permissions for admins, agents, and staff.

Activity Tracking

Daily task lists, call history, bulletin boards. Know exactly what happened today — and what needs to happen tomorrow.
