Features
Everything your agency needs.
Nothing it doesn't.
Built by someone who actually works in an insurance agency. Every feature exists because it solves a real daily problem.

WebRTC Calling

Click-to-dial from CJX, answer on your desk phone. Call logging, ringback, bridge, and mute — all built in.

SMS Messaging

Two-way texting with clients from your agency number. Full conversation history and quick replies. No personal phones.

Quick Quote Queue

Incoming quote requests land in a shared queue. Claim, assign, and track — no more lost sticky notes or email chains.

Contact Notes & CRM

Every call, every note, every policy detail — attached to the client. Full context for whoever picks up the phone next.

Team Management

Real-time availability, on-call indicators, internal messaging, and role-based permissions for admins, agents, and staff.

Enterprise Security

Two-factor authentication, role-based permissions, encrypted data, audit logging, and account lockout protection.
